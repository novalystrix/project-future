## Key Analytical Themes

**The Universal Bubble Sequence:**
Every bubble follows the same 5 stages: Displacement → Boom → Euphoria/Mania → Distress → Revulsion/Panic. The trigger is never the cause. The balloon is always the same; only the pin differs.

**What Makes Bubbles Catastrophic:**
The single most important variable is banking system exposure. Dot-com: equity-funded, banks unaffected → mild recession. 2008 housing: bank-funded, highly leveraged → near-depression. Japan: bank-funded, losses hidden for 15 years → 30-year stagnation.

**The AI Disruption Thesis:**
The SaaS multiple compression of 2022–2026 is Stage 1 of a potentially much larger disruption. The market is pricing a future where AI threatens white-collar knowledge work at scale. If that future arrives, the transmission mechanism to economic crisis runs through: employment → consumer credit → banking system. The historical precedents suggest severe but potentially manageable disruption if policy response is adequate.
