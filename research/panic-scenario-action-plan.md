## Stage 1 — Early Warning (We're Here Now)

**Signs:** AI is replacing knowledge workers faster than anyone expected. Layoffs in tech, media, finance. Unemployment trending up in white-collar sectors. Most people think it's a "tech thing."

### Do This Now

- [ ] **Buy physical gold** — 5% of your investable assets. Coins or small bars from a licensed dealer (Gold.co.il, Tavex, Bank Leumi). Store at home or in a bank vault.
- [ ] **Open a Swedish bank account** — Your wife opens it as a Swedish citizen. Swedbank or SEB. Fund with ~$15K equivalent in SEK. This is your EU financial beachhead.

**That's it for Stage 1. Four things.**

## Stage 2 — Slowdown Hits (12–24 months out)

**Signs:** Consumer spending drops. Office real estate values collapsing. Startup funding drying up. Tech unemployment normalized — now spreading to finance, legal, marketing. People are nervous but still in denial.

### Do This

- [ ] **Build full gold position** — bring it to 15% of investable assets. Mix of physical (10%) and gold ETF via IBKR (5%).

**Four things.**

## Stage 3 — Crisis Breaks (the Panic)**

**Signs:** Multiple regional bank failures in one quarter. S&P 500 down 30%+ from peak. VIX above 40. Government announces emergency measures. People are scared. Assets are cheap.

### Do This

- [ ] **Deploy 30–50% of your cash reserve** — Buy quality AI infra stocks at panic prices. NVDA, TSM, AVGO. Add energy/power plays (VST, CEG) — AI data centers need massive power.
- [ ] **Buy Bitcoin** — Self-custody only (Ledger/Trezor hardware wallet). Don't hold on exchanges. Target: 8–10% of portfolio. Bitcoin will crash first in the panic — that's when you buy it.
- [ ] **Close on the farm** — Farmland prices hold value in every historical collapse. If you haven't bought yet, this is the moment. People need to eat.
- [ ] **Activate Swedish banking** — Make sure the SEK account is funded. Prepare the family optionally to move if Israel's banking system shows stress.

**Four things.**

## Stage 4 — Government Response (6–18 months after crisis)

**Signs:** Governments announce UBI or massive stimulus. Central banks printing money. Inflation starting to appear after the deflationary crash. Assets starting to recover.

### Do This

- [ ] **Deploy remaining cash** — Inflation is coming. Deploy the rest of your cash reserve. Real assets, energy stocks, Bitcoin — they all outperform in inflation.
- [ ] **Hold your apartments** — Israeli residential real estate has structural support. Don't sell. In inflation, your fixed-rate mortgage becomes a gift (you're paying it back with cheaper money).
- [ ] **Rebalance crypto** — If Bitcoin is recovering, rebalance to target. This is the currency debasement phase where crypto was designed to win.
- [ ] **Consider Swedish property** — Optional, long-term. Rural Swedish property as Plan C. Your wife's citizenship makes it simple.

**Four things.**

## What NOT to Do (Ever)

| Don't | Why |
|-------|-----|
| Sell your apartments | Israeli housing has a floor. Rents may dip but the asset holds. You still have a roof. |
| Hold large cash in one Israeli bank | Deposit insurance is only ₪100K per person per bank. Spread it. |
| Buy variable-rate debt | In a crisis, variable debt can double overnight. Lock in fixed. |
| Buy speculative AI startups | Most will die. Own the picks-and-shovels (chips, power, data centers), not the hype. |
| Try to time the bottom | You won't. Dollar-cost average. Aim for "great" not "perfect." |
| Panic sell crypto | It will crash 50–70% first. That's expected. You're holding for the currency debasement phase that follows. |
